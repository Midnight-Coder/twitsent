We are conducting a study about brand perception that is reflected in discussion on twitter.

As an example, we are focusing on a telecommunications company, Orange, which is a branch of France Telecom

You will be presented with a series of tweets, each followed by several questions that aim to assess the relevance of the tweet and how it reflects the tweeter's sentiment.

About us: We are a research group at USC's AIL and SAIL labs, which comprises social scientists and engineers. We proudly support open source and dissemination of our results

Mechanical Turkers: we verify the quality of the results using randomly presented indicator stimuli. However, we select Turkers with high past acceptance, so in practice we reject very few HITs.
